Depression is not just a joke , depression is a disorder that affects the mood of the person that has continuous sadness and the loss of interest in things that they would like to pursue.
We aim to lessen the depression of all the people inthe world.
We aim to stop all things that result to depression like cyberbullying.
We also aim to help the people solve their problem because problems is the main reason for depression.


The aid to this disorder is therapy, therapy has a very important role to the people suffering from depression.
This helps them to feel relaxed and make them comfortable on the things that they loss interest.
One more key to overcome depression is antidepressant pills, antidepressant pills are pills that a psychiatric doctor gives to a patient that is suffering from depression.
This kind of medicine helps the patient calm their nerves to feel relaxed and calm.

Friends can be a good example of curing depression.
Real friends to be exact, because there is that one person that can make a depressed patient comfortable on the things that he/she wants to do or pursue.
A person that he/she can trust on telling their problems.

Meditation can also help you.
Meditation helps you to clear up your thoughts and stay calm.
It can reduce your negative thoughts because it helps you focus on one thing.
When negative thoughts came knocking, our brain returns to its focus point, allowing the negative emotions to pass.

Exercising can help you because it can give mood boosting effects.
When you exercise, your body releases chemicals called endorphins.
Endorphins also trigger a positive feeling in the body.
That's why for some people exercising acts as a antidepressant.

If the depression gets worse, See a psychiatrist or any licensed clinical worker to use some techniques to help you transpose your negative thoughts.
